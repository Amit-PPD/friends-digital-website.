Our story

From a Delhi print shop in 1981 to a national print powerhouse

Founded by Suresh Kumar Gupta in 1981, Friends Digital earned its name in the Delhi printing fraternity the old way — quality, honesty and delivery you could set your watch by.

Today the second generation — Mohinder Gupta, Amit Gupta and Nitin Gupta — leads one of India's largest printing units: 12+ production-class presses across digital, offset and large format, multiple facilities in Delhi NCR, and a trained team running them 24×7.

For more than 30 years we have served government institutions and won government tenders and contracts — work that demands documentation, discipline and delivery without excuses. That standard is the standard every client gets.
